About the company

For clients with intellectual disabilities, we provide staff nurses, home sitters, babysitters, and
Respite services. We are committed to delivering services designed to support the ability of each client to live safely
and as independently as possible in their home while offering help and peace of mind to their
family and loved ones. Our dedicated staff has demonstrated competency, has successfully completed written and
practical skill training and has a strong desire to assist clients in reaching their goals.
We can provide a no-obligation “needs assessment visit” to help you decide the best choice for
the client and to discuss the available options for the plan of care
